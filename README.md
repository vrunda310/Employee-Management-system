#  Employee Management System

This repository contains the **Employee Management & Learning Portal**, a centralized system designed to manage employee information, learning content, and internal company resources in one place.

The project follows a **headless architecture**, where content and data are managed centrally and consumed by a frontend application.

---

##  Project Overview

The Employee Management System helps organizations streamline internal operations by providing a single platform for employee data, learning management, and company communication, while ensuring **role-based access** and **usability for non-technical users**.

---

##  Core Features

- Employee data management  
- Learning and training content management  
- Course assignment and progress tracking  
- Company policies, notices, and document management  
- Company events, calendar, and news declarations  
- Important announcements management  
- Unit locations and route information management  
- Role-based access control (Employee, HR, Learning Manager, Admin)  
- Secure REST APIs for frontend consumption
